Research Goals and Interests

My long-term goal is to develop a microbiome-informed agronomy taking into account the evolutionary and ecological complexity of plant-microbiota interactions.

Some of my interests include:

  • The rhizosphere effect and plant immune interactions with microbiota.
  • The symbiosis spectrum of the root microbiome: pathogens, commensals, and mutualists.
  • Microbiome dynamics specific to agricultural systems.
